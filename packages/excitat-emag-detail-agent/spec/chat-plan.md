# Chat Plan Template

## Buyer Question Order

1. What result do I get?
2. Does it fit my situation?
3. Why should I believe the claim?
4. How do I use it?
5. What do I receive?
6. What objections remain?
7. Can I trust the brand or seller context?

## Copy Policy

- Write buyer-facing proof, not internal SOP.
- Keep unsupported claims as angles or questions.
- Do not invent review, rating, service, warranty, return, or shipping facts.

# EXCITAT eMAG Detail Design System v1.1

## Brand Rule

- Display brand: `EXCITAT`.
- Forbidden display names: `EXIT`, `Excité`, `Exceity`, `EXITE`, `BestPlaza`.
- Brand wordmark should be large but never compete with product evidence.
- Use a small EXCITAT tag when the banner is product-led; use a large EXCITAT wordmark only in brand-led modules.

## Visual Rhythm

Premium detail pages are not a sequence of heavy cards. Use rhythm:

```text
wide scene banner -> short white text -> cropped proof -> clean specs/QA -> review or trust -> brand closer
```

Rules:

- Use one heavy dark module per page segment.
- Do not repeat a full product image immediately after a product-scene hero.
- Crop source images into proof details: connector, button, material, capacity, package, use action.
- Use white or clean light modules as visual rest.
- Small boxes are allowed for specs, manual steps, comparison and first-screen anchors only.

## Color Families

- Tech / EV: black, deep navy, electric blue, cyan, small green.
- Baby / kitchen: warm white, cream, light green, soft orange, small cyan.
- Brand trust: black or deep charcoal with gold/orange linework.
- QA / review stable boards: clean white/light gray or restrained dark; never dense promotional clutter.

## Typography

- Brand word: largest visual type, but only in brand modules.
- Module title: 34-56 px on 1140-wide rendered images.
- Supporting text: 20-26 px, 1-3 lines.
- Small chips: exact text only, locally rendered.
- Model-generated small text is not allowed.

## Motion

Stable:

- `shine_sweep`: black-gold/blue highlight passing across a static image.
- `light_trail`: subtle line behind product or brand.
- `step_highlight`: numbered steps light up one at a time.
- `icon_pulse`: small icon attention cue.

Experimental:

- people micro-motion
- product rotation
- product edge glow
- particle field
- breathing border

Downgrade to static if motion deforms the product or reduces mobile readability.
